Alice in Borderland

Overview

*Alice in Borderland* is a Japanese sci-fi thriller series based on the manga by Haro Aso. It follows Arisu and his friends, who find themselves in a deserted version of Tokyo where they must participate in deadly games to survive.

Key Information

  • Genre: Sci-Fi, Thriller, Survival
  • Original Release: December 10, 2020
  • Created by: Shinsuke Sato
  • Streaming on: Netflix
