Critical Reviews

Season 3 Announcement and Series Overview

Netflix's "Alice in Borderland" has been confirmed for a third season, following its initial announcement in September 2023. The live-action series, based on Haro Aso's manga, gained worldwide popularity, with its second season breaking viewership records. The series follows Ryohei Arisu (Kento Yamazaki) and Yuzuha Usagi (Tao Tsuchiya) as they navigate a parallel universe where survival depends on winning deadly games. Season 2 concluded with them returning to the real world after defeating the Face Cards and a cliffhanger revealing the Joker card, hinting that the games may not be over. Season 3 promises new challenges and deeper exploration of themes like humanity and sacrifice. Yamazaki and Tsuchiya will reprise their roles, and the storyline is expected to maintain its intensity under the direction of Shinsuke Sato. The release date for the new season has not yet been announced.

Source: People Magazine
