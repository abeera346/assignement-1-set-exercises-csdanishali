Plot

"Alice in Borderland" follows Ryohei Arisu, a listless, jobless, and video-game-obsessed young man who suddenly finds himself in a strange, emptied-out version of Tokyo. In this dystopian setting, he and his friends must compete in dangerous games to survive. Along the way, Arisu meets Yuzuha Usagi, a young woman navigating the games alone. Together, they set out to unravel one mystery after another, risking their lives and confronting what it means to live.
