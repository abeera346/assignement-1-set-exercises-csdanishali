Main Characters

  • Ryohei Arisu: A disillusioned young man with a passion for video games. He finds himself in a deserted Tokyo where he must participate in deadly games to survive.
  • Yuzuha Usagi: A skilled mountain climber who becomes Arisu's ally. She relies on her physical prowess and survival instincts to navigate the challenges.
  • Shuntaro Chishiya: A cunning and strategic player who often operates alone. His calm demeanor hides a calculating mind.
  • Hikari Kuina: A resilient woman with a complex past. She partners with Chishiya and showcases exceptional combat skills.
  • Aguni Morizono: A former soldier and leader of a militant group in the Borderlands. He struggles with his own moral compass amidst the chaos.
